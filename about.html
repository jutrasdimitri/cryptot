BTC

BITCOIN

VIRES IN NUMERIS // Strength in Numbers

Get The Tee

What Bitcoin Is — Straight to the Point

Bitcoin is a peer-to-peer electronic cash system designed to operate without trust.

Its core purpose is simple:

  • fixed supply
  • decentralized validation
  • permissionless value transfer

Bitcoin exists to solve one problem: money that cannot be inflated, censored, or controlled.

The Core Problem — Infinite Money vs Finite Resources

Traditional money is created by central banks. It has: infinite supply, political control, and hidden inflation.

Bitcoin introduced the opposite: a hard cap of 21 million, predictable issuance, and no authority capable of printing more. This transforms money from debt into property.

Byzantine Generals Problem — Solved

Before Bitcoin, decentralized money failed because of the Byzantine Generals Problem: How do independent actors agree without trusting each other?

Bitcoin solved this with Proof of Work, economic incentives, and cryptographic verification. Consensus is not based on trust. It is based on math, energy, and game theory.

Satoshi Nakamoto — The Disappearance That Mattered

Bitcoin was introduced in 2008 by Satoshi Nakamoto, a pseudonymous creator whose true identity remains unknown.

There was no premine, no founder allocation, and no central leadership. This anonymity is not a weakness. It is a feature. It ensures no authority figure, no single point of failure, and no one to pressure, arrest, or influence.

Bitcoin belongs to no one. And therefore, to everyone.

Original website: bitcoin.org

The Bitcoin Community

Bitcoin is more than software. It is a culture. The Bitcoin community is known for extreme conviction, long-term thinking, and resistance to narratives and hype.

Bitcoin Maximalists believe Bitcoin is the only truly decentralized money, everything else is either experimental or compromised, and security matters more than speed.

To outsiders, it looks like a cult. To insiders, it is discipline.

Bitcoin Timeline — Key Events

  • 2008 — Whitepaper released by Satoshi Nakamoto
  • 2009 — Genesis Block mined
  • 2010 — First purchase (10,000 BTC for pizza)
  • 2012 — First halving (50 → 25 BTC)
  • 2017 — Mainstream awareness & futures markets
  • 2020 — Institutional accumulation begins
  • 2021 — Legal tender in El Salvador
  • 2024 — Fourth halving & Spot ETFs

Wake up. Bitcoin is going to infinite.

ETH

ETHEREUM

CODE IS LAW // Programmable Money

Get The Tee

What Ethereum Is — In One Sentence

Ethereum is a decentralized global computing platform that allows anyone to deploy unstoppable applications and programmable money without intermediaries.

If Bitcoin proved that money can exist without banks, Ethereum proved that logic, contracts, and entire financial systems can exist without companies.

Why Ethereum Was Created

Bitcoin solved money. But a limitation remained: value could be transferred, but behavior could not be programmed. Ethereum was created to answer a single question: What if the blockchain itself could run code?

Smart Contracts — Explained Simply

A smart contract is code deployed on the blockchain. Once deployed, it cannot be altered, it cannot be stopped, and it executes automatically.

Use cases include: decentralized exchanges, lending protocols, stablecoins, NFTs, and DAOs. Ethereum transforms the blockchain into a programmable settlement layer.

The Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM)

Ethereum applications run inside the EVM. It executes identically on thousands of nodes and guarantees the same result worldwide. This makes Ethereum censorship-resistant and globally synchronized. It is often described as the world computer.

Vitalik Buterin — Creator

Vitalik Buterin grew up in Toronto. At age 19, he wrote the Ethereum whitepaper (2013) because he recognized Bitcoin’s scripting limitations. Unlike Satoshi, Vitalik is public, research-driven, and focused on long-term evolution.

The Ethereum Community

Ethereum’s community values experimentation, composability, and developer freedom. Rather than maximalism, Ethereum embraces Layer 2 scaling and constant upgrades. Ethereum does not aim to be perfect. It aims to evolve.

Timeline

  • 2013 — Whitepaper published by Vitalik
  • 2015 — Mainnet launch
  • 2016 — The DAO hack
  • 2017 — ICO Boom (ERC-20)
  • 2020 — DeFi Summer
  • 2022 — The Merge (Proof of Stake)

Bitcoin replaced money. Ethereum replaces institutions.

ADA

CARDANO

PEER REVIEWED // The Bitcoin of Altcoins

Get The Tee

What Cardano Is

Cardano is a peer-to-peer blockchain built on the same core accounting philosophy as Bitcoin (UTXO), but extending it to support smart contracts and governance. It is not a departure from Bitcoin; it is a continuation.

This is why Cardano is often called: “The Bitcoin of Altcoins.”

The Philosophy: Measure Twice, Cut Once

Cardano is built on peer-reviewed academic research, formal methods, and mathematical proofs. Instead of “move fast and break things,” Cardano chose correctness over speed and safety over hype.

Charles Hoskinson

Charles was an early Bitcoin educator and a co-founder of Ethereum. He left Ethereum due to philosophical differences regarding governance and structure. He believed blockchains should be built like critical infrastructure—meant to last decades.

Proof of Stake — Ouroboros

Cardano uses Ouroboros, the first Proof of Stake protocol proven secure through peer-reviewed research. It is energy efficient, mathematically proven, and maintains decentralization.

Real-World Vision

Cardano focuses on digital identity, government-grade infrastructure, and financial inclusion (especially in Africa). It is designed to scale to billions of people who lack access to banks.

Community: "In Charles We Trust"

The community is known for patience and a long-term mindset. They don't follow Charles Hoskinson for price predictions, but for his vision of structure and sovereignty. It isn't blind faith; it is alignment with a philosophy.

Timeline

  • 2015 — Research begins
  • 2017 — Mainnet launch
  • 2020 — Shelley era (Decentralization)
  • 2021 — Alonzo upgrade (Smart Contracts)
  • 2024 — Voltaire era (On-chain governance)

In Charles we trust. You are early.

XRP

XRP

THE STANDARD // Global Settlement

Get The Tee

What XRP Is

XRP is a digital asset designed for fast, low-cost, cross-border value transfer. Its purpose is simple: Move value globally, instantly, and at scale.

Bitcoin challenges money itself. XRP fixes how money moves. XRP is not anti-bank. XRP is post-SWIFT.

The Problem: Legacy Finance

SWIFT was created in the 1970s. It doesn't move money; it sends messages. It takes days to settle. XRP settles in 3-5 seconds with finality.

XRP Ledger vs. Ripple

Ripple is a private company building solutions for banks. The XRP Ledger (XRPL) is decentralized and open-source. Ripple does not control the network. This distinction is critical.

The SEC Lawsuit & Victory

In Dec 2020, the SEC sued Ripple, claiming XRP was a security. The price collapsed, but the community ("The XRP Army") stood firm.

In July 2023, the court ruled: XRP itself is NOT a security. This was a landmark victory for the entire industry.

The XRP Army

XRP is backed by one of the most resilient communities in crypto. They researched, they organized, and they refused to sell. XRP survived not because of hype, but because of conviction.

Timeline

  • 2012 — XRPL launched
  • 2013 — Ripple Labs founded
  • 2017 — XRP enters top 3 assets
  • 2020 — SEC Lawsuit filed
  • 2023 — Court ruling: Not a Security
  • 2025 — Global settlement standard

The Standard is changing.

SOL

SOLANA

PROOF OF HISTORY // Speed as Primitive

Get The Tee

What Solana Is

Solana is a high-performance Layer 1 designed to scale to global usage. Its mission: Deliver blockchain-level decentralization at internet speed.

Bitcoin introduced scarcity. Ethereum introduced programmability. Solana introduced time.

History Is The Proof

Solana introduced Proof of History (PoH). It is a cryptographic clock that allows the network to order events before consensus. This drastically reduces latency. Time is already proven.

Outages & Resilience

Solana experienced outages due to extreme load and bot activity. Instead of abandoning the project, the engineers hardened the network. By 2024, stability dramatically improved. Speed was not abandoned; it was refined.

Mobile & Consumer Focus

Solana targets end users with initiatives like the Solana Phone (Saga) and an integrated mobile stack. The goal is to bring crypto to normal users, not just crypto natives.

Timeline

  • 2017 — Solana founded
  • 2020 — Mainnet launch
  • 2021 — Explosive DeFi growth
  • 2022 — Market crash & rebuild
  • 2023 — Network hardening
  • 2024 — Consumer & Mobile resurgence

Speed is not a feature. It is the foundation.
