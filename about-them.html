Limited Supply Is not an Option

It Is The Point

Every fiat currency has failed for the same reason: infinite supply controlled by the few.

As soon as money can be printed at will, it ceases to be a store of value. It becomes a tool of extraction.

Savings are diluted. Labor is devalued. Time is stolen.

This is not a bug in the system. It is the system.

Bitcoin Changed One Rule

And That Changed Everything

Bitcoin introduced two ideas simultaneously:

1. A fixed, immutable supply
2. Peer-to-peer transfer without intermediaries

Not separately. Together.

Money that cannot be printed and cannot be censored.

No central bank. No third-party permission. No dilution.

For the first time in modern history, money became scarce by code, not by trust.

Bitcoin does not promise returns. It reveals the flaw in fiat.
